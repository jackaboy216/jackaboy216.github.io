The Crew

Redline Rebels started with 2 friends and the adrenaline of the open road. We’re more than just a car crew—we’re a family built on the thrill of the chase. From underground street meets to the heat of the track, we push our builds to the limit and leave everything on the asphalt.

Our lineup is a mix of everything that moves—JDM legends, refined Euro tuners, and raw American muscle. We don’t care where your car was built, as long as you’ve got the passion to drive it. Every car has a story, and every member has a voice; together, we’re making the streets our canvas, one gear at a time.

Catch Us Out There

We don't just stay in the garage. We’re active in the scene, rolling out to the city's best meets, night runs, and local shows.
